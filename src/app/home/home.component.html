What Linda does ?

In order to allow biologists to capture and store the environmental metadata associated with plant phenotyping experiments and to facilitate a standardized exchange of information, the LINDA Web interface helps users to insert their data according to the MIAPPE model and supported by all related ontologies (Plant ontology, XEO, ENVo, etc.). The MIAPPE requirements should serve as a checklist for the researchers recording the data to make them consider all aspects that might influence the experimental process and take note of those aspects. Exporting to ISA-tab format will allow subsequent interconnection with FAIR compliance so that all metadata can be linked to existing experiments and other MIAPPE formatted databases. The final objective of LINDA is to correlate together data and metadata from upstream and downstream analyses to data from downstream metabolomics assays with upstream experimental/environmental data/metadata, Linda will significantly facilitate phenotypes and metabolism mechanisms understanding more easily

  • Describes observation unit and climatic events using ontology
