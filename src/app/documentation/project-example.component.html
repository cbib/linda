Model project

Dans cet exemple, nous allons étudier une variété de tomates (Ida Gold) cultivées en plein air dans des parcelles situé sur le site de L'INRA BFP.

First, we will describe the entire experimental design using the MIAPPE forms available to you. In a second step, we will describe the metadata associated with the experience and the entry of this metadata. The culture will take place from March 15 to August 15, 2021. In total, 5 observation points will be established, one per month (April 15, May 15, June 15, July 15 and August 15). At this observation point, we will take 5 leaf samples which will be measured. Several experimental factors will be taken into account, the soil moisture rate, the nitrogen rate in the growing medium, the sunshine on the plot (lit / in the shade).

Biological Material

A total of 72 plants will be cultivated, 9 per condition (plot). the size of the land is 10 m by 5 m. Each plot measures 1.80 m by 1.80 m.

Experimental Design

Growth protocol

The plants are treated with different rates of nitrogen. a low rate (2%) and a high rate (10%)

Facteurs expérimentaux

The plants are grown under different conditions and treatment. These experimental factors will be described using the corresponding form. factor

Add investigation

First of all, you need to create an investigation, just click on the associated and a menu will appear from which you can choose between three proposals

Three choices are available to the user

  • Add investigation from original model template

    The MIAPPE model form for an investigation. The description of the fields for each structure of the MIAPPE model can be found here

  • Add investigation from saved template

    The user can save a pre-filled template by checking the save template option when validating the form. These pre-filled templates can then be reused as many times as needed. However, it is rare to create the same investigation twice but it can be useful to replay type / model studies in other investigations

  • Add investigation from zip files

    The user can save the whole of an investigation with all the elements composing it as a compressed folder in .zip format. Therefore, the user can also upload an entire investigation as .zip files.

At this point, we choose to start from the empty template because we haven't saved any templates. there are 8 fields to fill in to describe your investigation. The first two are mandatory and must be unique.

Once the form is completed, you can choose to save it as a template, in order to be able to reuse it later as a template in other projects, by activating the option save as template at the top right. Then, click on the Submit button to add your investigation to the investigation tree.

Describe your Study

The investigation is the highest level of the MIAPPE hierarchy and may contain different studies within it. In our case, we are going to create a single study that we will name "tomato1_study1". The form for describing a study contains 20 fields to fill in Study . Some fields are mandatory, such as the study identifier, which must be unique.

Describe your material

Germplasm informations

To describe a biological material, the MiAPPE standard requires to describe at least the studied organism, the genus, the species and the name of the sub-taxa if they exist.

Describe your material source

The original source of that material (e.g., the seeds or the original plant cloned) is called the material source, which, when held by a material repository, should have its stock identified The material source must be identified with a unique identifier. Other information is used to describe various characteristics of the material.
